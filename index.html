Our Story

Meet Your Baker

Hello! I'm Lijina Sajeev, he heart and hands behind this home-based cake studio, where every creation is made with passion, care, and a deep love for baking. What started as a simple joy in the kitchen has grown into a meaningful journey of turning everyday ingredients into beautiful, memorable cakes that bring smiles to people’s faces.

Each cake I bake is crafted from scratch using fresh, high-quality ingredients. I believe that great taste comes from honesty—no shortcuts, no unnecessary preservatives, just pure, homemade goodness. From mixing the batter to the final decorative touch, every step is done with attention to detail and dedication.

Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, wedding, or a small personal celebration, I strive to create cakes that not only look stunning but also taste unforgettable. I understand that every occasion is special, and my goal is to make it even more meaningful with a cake that reflects your story and style. — Made with Love.
